# Char

## Types

Each class has a mix of these 10 types.  

#### Physical
- Sword
- Polearm
- Knife
- Axe
- Staff

#### Magical
- Fire
- Ice
- Lightning
- Holy
- Dark

## Stats

Statistics will be from 0-15 scale just like Baseball Stars.  

Includes:
- P.Atk
- P.Def
- M.Atk
- M.Def
- Speed
- Stamina
- Prestige

## Strengths and Weaknesses
Each class can have up to five strengths and five weaknesses.
